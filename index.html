About Michele

Licensed Clinical Social Worker & Supervisor

A Warm, Professional Approach to Healing

Life can be both beautiful and challenging, and at times it may feel overwhelming to navigate on your own. If you or your child are struggling with anxiety, trauma, grief, or major life transitions, you don't have to face it alone. Taking the first step toward healing can be difficult—but it can also open the door to meaningful change.

As a Licensed Clinical Social Worker, I specialize in working with children, individuals, and families, with a strong focus on trauma-informed care. I am passionate about supporting clients from diverse and BIPOC communities, offering a culturally responsive and affirming space.

"My goal is to create a supportive, trusting therapeutic relationship where you feel understood, meet you where you are, and help you move toward where you want to be."

I provide a supportive space to help you move forward. I help clients better understand their experiences, build practical coping skills, and develop tools they can use in everyday life. Together, we'll work to reduce distress, strengthen relationships, and build resilience so you can feel more confident and grounded.

Healing and self-reflection can be powerful—and at times, challenging—which is why finding the right connection matters.
